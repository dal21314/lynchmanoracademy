I  ·  The Three Pillars

A Threefold Foundation

Every lesson at Lynch Manor stands on these three words.

I✦

Scientia

Knowledge

Rigorous academics aligned to Pennsylvania Academic Standards, taught with curiosity and real-world connection. A structured 180-day year across reading, writing, math, science, social studies, art, and music.

II✦

Virtus

Virtue

Character formation grounded in a Christian worldview. Each lesson closes with scripture relevant to the day's learning — a foundation of faith built alongside academic excellence.

III✦

Lumen

Light

Hands-on learning drawn from our family flower farm, the seasons, and the world around us. Knowledge becomes wisdom when it meets real life.

II  ·  About the Academy

A Home, A Schoolhouse, A Farm

Lynch Manor Academy is the home education program founded by the Lynch family in Pennsylvania. LMA exists to give our children — and the children who follow — a rigorous, faith-rooted education that takes both the classical academic tradition and the real world seriously.

Our classroom is part schoolhouse, part flower farm, part kitchen table. We teach by Pennsylvania Academic Standards, integrate a Christian worldview, and build our own curriculum where the best off-the-shelf options fall short.
